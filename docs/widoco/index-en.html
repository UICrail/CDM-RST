Coachc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#Coach

TSI LOCPAS 2020: a vehicle without traction in a fixed or variable formation capable of carrying passengers (by extension, requirements specified to apply to coaches in this TSI are deemed to apply also to restaurant cars, sleeping cars, couchettes cars, etc.) Note: was "passenger carriage" in earlier versions.
has super-classes
Passenger Carrying Rolling Stock c, Vehicle c

Empty listc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://w3id.org/list#EmptyList

Closing element of any List under the List ontology. This element does not correspond with any rolling stock.
is in range of
has next rolling stock op
is disjoint with
Listed rolling stock c

Extremityc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/cons/cons#Extremity

denotes the extremity of a rolling stock. The extremity designation is either set at design and manufacturing time (e.g. extremity1, extremity 2 in the case of a wagon), or rests on other conventions (e.g. extremities of a variable-consist trainset).
has super-classes
is in domain of
faces extremity op
is in range of
faces extremity op, front extremity op, has extremity op

Locomotivec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#Locomotive

Powered vehicle having a driving desk and can be used standalone (e.g. for shunting or ferrying movements), but is normally used for hauling other vehicles or formations.
has super-classes
Motored Rolling Stock c, Vehicle c

Motor Carc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#MotorCar

Powered vehicle that cannot be used standalone, as can be found in trainset (EMUs or DMUs). May or may not carry passengers.
has super-classes
Motored Rolling Stock c, Vehicle c

Motored Rolling Stockc back to ToC or Class ToC

Rolling Stock Feature c
has sub-classes
Locomotive c, Motor Car c, On Track Machine c, Rail Car c, Slave Locomotive c, Trainset c

Rail Carc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#RailCar

Wikipedia (12/3/2024): self-propelled railway vehicle designed to transport passengers. Equiv. to FR: Autorail, DE: Schienenbus (narrower meaning: two axles only). Note however the numerous variants and ambiguities.
has super-classes
Motored Rolling Stock c, Passenger Carrying Rolling Stock c, Vehicle c

Slave Locomotivec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#SlaveLocomotive

Powered vehicle without driving desk, with energy possibly fed from master locomotive ( = subclass "slug"). Also called "truck" in France, e.g. TC61000. Do not confuse with master/slave configuration of two normal locomotives ("multiple working" in the UK).
has super-classes
Motored Rolling Stock c, Vehicle c

Trailerc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#Trailer

non-traction, passenger-carrying vehicle that is part of a trainset or of a fixed-consist rake, such as TGV trailer or ICE3 trailer or Talgo trailer.
has super-classes
Passenger Carrying Rolling Stock c, Vehicle c

Trainsetc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#Trainset

TSI LOC&PAS, 2020: a fixed formation that can operate as a train; it is by definition not intended to be reconfigured, except within a workshop environment. It is composed of only motored or of motored and non-motored vehicles.
has super-classes
Formation c, Motored Rolling Stock c

Vanc back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/typo/typo#Van

TSI LOCPAS 2020: a vehicle without traction capable of carrying payload other than passengers, e.g. luggage or mail, intended to be integrated into a fixed or variable formation which is intended to transport passengers.
has super-classes
Freight Carrying Rolling Stock c, Vehicle c

coupled toop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/cons/cons#coupledTo

Symmetric property "coupled to" relates two extremities of two distinct Rolling stock items (hence the "to" in the label). The property is expressed irrespectively of the type of coupling (mechanical, virtual) or coupler (UIC, Scharfenberg...).

has characteristics: functional, inverse functional, symmetric, irreflexive

has super-properties
faces extremity op

has head rolling stockop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/cons/cons#hasHeadRollingStock

Each formation has exactly one "First" rolling stock. This notion of "first" is only related to the rolling stock, not however to the direction of travel which may vary (general case of multiple units or reversible trains) or not (case of hauled freight trains). Please also not that the direction of movement is yet another notion (think of shunting).

has characteristics: functional, inverse functional

has super-properties
includes Rolling stock op
has domain
Formation c
has range
Listed rolling stock c
