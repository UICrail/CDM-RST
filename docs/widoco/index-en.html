coupled toop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/cons#coupledTo

Symmetric property "coupled to" relates two extremities of two distinct Rolling stock items (hence the "to" in the label). The property is expressed irrespectively of the type of coupling (mechanical, virtual) or coupler (UIC, Scharfenberg...).

has characteristics: functional, inverse functional, symmetric, irreflexive

has super-properties
faces extremity op

has head rolling stockop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/cons#hasHeadRollingStock

Each formation has exactly one "First" rolling stock. This notion of "first" is only related to the rolling stock, not however to the direction of travel which may vary (general case of multiple units or reversible trains) or not (case of hauled freight trains). Please also not that the direction of movement is yet another notion (think of shunting).

has characteristics: functional, inverse functional

has super-properties
includes Rolling stock op
has domain
Formation c
has range
Listed rolling stock c

includes Rolling stockop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/cons#includesRollingStock

This property describes the composition of a Formation. A Formation may incude any rolling stock (i.e. vehicles or formations). Irreflexive property: a formation cannot include itself; in other words, it may only have proper parts.

has characteristics: inverse functional, irreflexive

has sub-properties
has head rolling stock op
has domain
Formation c
has range
Rolling stock c
is inverse of
part of formation op

part of formationop back to ToC or Object Property ToC

IRI: https://cdm.ovh/rst/cons#partOfFormation

Inverse of "includes Rolling Stock". A functional property: a rolling stock can only be part of one formation. Irreflexive property: A formation cannot be part of itself.

has characteristics: functional, irreflexive

is inverse of
includes Rolling stock op
